720 at BofA - Advocacy for a Better Bus Stop

Why We Exist

We are a group of parents advocating for a safer and more convenient bus stop for our children. We aim to:

  • Move the morning bus stop to a safer location.
  • Ensure all parents arrive on time to avoid delays.
  • Work together to improve communication and accountability.

How You Can Help

We need support from parents in our community. Join us in signing the petition to move the bus stop and help hold each other accountable for punctuality.
